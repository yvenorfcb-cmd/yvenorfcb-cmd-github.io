        <strong>International Monetary Fund (IMF)</strong>, the <strong>World Bank</strong>, and the

        emerging economies. My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of policies that support

        emerging countries in building long-term economic resilience and sustainable growth as they integrate

        into the global economy.
